The Xposome Portal

Chemical Screening using high-throughput transcriptomics assays


Each of the chemical screen part of the Xposome can be queried through one of these modalities

Annotation

Provides description of each chemical profiled, including at which dose(s) and concentration(s), as well as reported adverse effects associated with exposure to it.

Heatmap Explorer

A heatmap visualizer to explore gene expression, gene set enrichment, and connectivity results across genes and chemicals. Interactive visualization based on Morpheus.

Marker Explorer

Interactive explorer for a single queried markers (gene, gene-set, or CMap perturbagen/perturbagen class).

K2Taxonomer

Interactive visualization of the taxonomic hierarchy induced by the transcriptional responses to chemicals in a screen. Supports the gene- and geset-based querying and comparison of chemical groups’ transcriptional responses. Based on K2Taxonomer.

Chemical Explorer

For any screened chemical, queries can return: i) normalized measure of each gene expression response; ii) geneset enrichment scores measuring the coordinated response of the genes in a geneset or pathway to the chemical; or iii) the chemical connectivity of (i.e., similarity of the response to those of) chemicals or class of chemicals profiled in the CMap.
